First Timer FAQ

Common questions before your first punch.

Is it hard?

It's challenging, but you control the intensity. Instructors provide modifications for every fitness level.

What should I wear?

Athletic wear that you can move freely in. You won't wear shoes on the mats, so socks or barefoot is fine (check studio policy).

How long is class?

Most classes are 45 to 60 minutes long, including warm-up, bag work, and cool-down.
